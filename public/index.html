Elbie is a Discord bot designed to run basic rules-lite TTRPGs by automating skill rolls, levelling, and a host of other stuff that keeps you looking at your sheet instead of engaging in the game. Make a wisdom check with '+s wis' instead of having to remember your modifiers. Get a personalized shorthand character sheet with '+who'. A host of other commands are available too, with one feature that distinguishes her from the majority of discord bots: nothing gross.

To invite Elbeanor to your server, click here and select a server on which you have "Manage Server" permissions.
